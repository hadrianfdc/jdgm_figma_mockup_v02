A Mission Born From Faith,
Built on Real Compassion

Founded in Suwanee, Georgia in 2022, JDGM is a boots-on-the-ground humanitarian organization operating in the Philippines and Uganda.

Our Story

From a Single Mission Trip to a Global Movement

In 2022, Pastor Johnny Davis traveled to Cebu, Philippines and witnessed firsthand the devastating effects of child hunger and poverty. He returned to Georgia with a mission: build a structured, transparent organization that could provide sustained relief — not just one-time charity.

Today, JDGM has 157 active volunteers, has raised $45,624 in donations, and operates six distinct programs across the Philippines and Uganda with full financial transparency.

View Our ProgramsTransparency Report
⛪
Our Mission
To embody the compassion of Christ by meeting the physical and spiritual needs of the vulnerable — feeding the hungry, healing the sick, freeing the captive.
Our Vision
A world where every child has food, clean water, and access to education — and where faith organizations lead in accountability and impact measurement.
Our Values
Transparency · Accountability · Faith-driven action · Dignity for every person served · Long-term partnership over one-time charity.
Our Journey

How We Got Here

2022
JDGM Founded
Pastor Johnny registers JDGM as a 501(c)(3). First mission to Cebu, Philippines. 12 volunteers, 400 meals served.
2023
Programs Expand
Clean Water program launches. Wells #1 and #2 complete in Cebu. Anti-trafficking partnership established. Donor count reaches 50+.
2024
Uganda Work Begins
First medical mission to Uganda. Education sponsorship launches. $45,624 raised. 157 active volunteers. 100+ charitable donors.
2025
Rebuilding for Impact
New platform launch. "Compassion Circle" monthly giving program. Google for Nonprofits grant application in progress.
